10,000

How to Play:

  • Objective: Reach a score of 10,000 and have the most out of everybody else playing (in this case, just reaching a score of 10,000).
  • If a dice rolls a 5, it is worth 50.
  • If a dice rolls a 1, it is worth 100.
  • If three dice roll and get the same number, it is worth its hundred equivalent (ex. three dice show a number 5, it is worth 500).
  • This is an exception to the previous rule: three ones scores a 1000.
  • If all the dice roll and land from 1 through 6, it is worth 1500.
  • If you roll all the dice and they all land on 1s, it is worth 10,000.
